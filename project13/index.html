Kogi Taco Truck offers an innovative fusion of Korean and Mexican cuisines, serving up mouthwatering tacos and other dishes. It has gained widespread popularity for its unique flavors and lively street food vibe, becoming a staple of the food truck scene in Los Angeles and beyond.

Kogi Taco Truck seeks an illustrative design to re-activate their brand by visually engaging customers and revitalizing brand recognition.

*concept

.

I combined the ancient Korean traditional costume of hats with Mexican hats and cactus elements. Against a backdrop of fiery reds and oranges, the figures wear traditional ponchos or Mexican hats, and are dotted with cacti. The colors of the characters use green, purple and white as contrast to highlight the vivid visual effects.
