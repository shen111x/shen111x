The design stems from the brand's philosophy, conveying an existential worldview through the liquefaction of the human form. Internally, the object is hollow, with the sole logo discreetly placed within the invisible center of the void, symbolizing the brand's profound essence and prompting contemplation on existence and the unseen beauty within.
