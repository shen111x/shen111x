The Place is the Space Biennial celebrates 21st-century art, architecture, and design, exploring the intersection of land, art, and architecture. Through exhibitions, talks, and interventions, it inspires a new generation to reimagine architecture's role in society, fostering sustainability and modern dwelling.

Originating from a collaboration of artists, writers, and activists addressing climate change, social inequality, and environmental degradation, The Place is the Space Biennial is a weekend-long extravaganza showcasing large-scale interventions challenging conventional ideas about space and place.

*concept

.

I rekon what defines a place is the different in-side space ( interior ) that created by people. My concept explores the nuanced bond between space and human sociability, depicting diversity and unity through unique combinations of negative space and shapes.
