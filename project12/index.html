After thorough research on exhibiting artists, my focus gravitated towards the essence of "futuristic humanoids combined with virtual reality." In visually echoing participating artists, the poster directly recreates a "separated reality." Distorted font motion and dynamic background shifts elevate the poster's visual experience, seamlessly integrating it as an integral part of the exhibition itself.
