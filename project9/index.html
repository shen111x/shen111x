The theme of this art exhibition is anxiety, and my concept involves using controlled lighting to highlight specific areas while leaving all exhibited works in dark, unlit spaces. This approach aims to elevate the inherent emotions conveyed by the artworks, embracing an existential anxiety. Additionally, a peculiar vending machine and photographic pieces at the entrance set the tone for the exhibition, serving as integral tools to establish its ambiance.
