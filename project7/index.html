Blue Powder New York
Rebranding and Winter Sale Banner

.


Client: Blue Powder New York

Delivery: Jan 2024


Blue Powder" is a New York-based ski equipment store catering to the growing demographic of young skiing enthusiasts. Currently undergoing a rebranding initiative, the store aims to revitalize its image and better resonate with the preferences of the younger audience. The primary clientele of "Blue Powder" consists of the burgeoning community of skiing enthusiasts among the youth.

The rebranding project focuses on infusing the new design with a youthful and dynamic appeal, making it more engaging and aligned with the tastes and interests of the younger demographic. The client came with a specific request, expressing a desire to use the initials B and P to create an illustrative-style face as a secondary logo.

Blue Powder signifies the color of snow after a heavy fall, exhibiting a blue tint in sunlight. My design is trying to reflecting the love and passion for skiing.
