Dumpling Dang
Visual Identity

.


Client: City of ChangShu

Delivery: June 2019


Nestled in Suzhou's revitalized tourist hub, this dumpling haven, part of the snack street, is undergoing a visual rebrand. Specializing in dumplings and diverse Chinese delicacies, the compact eatery embraces its role within the bustling tourist area, offering a delectable array of quick bites to visitors exploring the revamped district.

Situated in Suzhou's redeveloped tourist zone, this dumpling eatery is a gem on the snack street. Specializing in delectable dumplings and a variety of Chinese dishes, it caters to visitors exploring the vibrant surroundings.

*concept

.

Recognizing the fast-casual nature of the eatery, where patrons spend limited time, the goal is to create memorable impressions through visually appealing elements. These details, including captivating colors and intricate illustrations, aim to leave a lasting imprint, ensuring customers remember the unique charm of the dumpling haven.
